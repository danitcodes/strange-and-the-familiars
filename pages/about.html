Stephanie Strange has been sharing her messages with Portland audiences for more than thirteen years and has been featured in Vortex Magazine, Eleven Magazine, Voyage LA, and on KBOO, XRAY.FM, PRP.FM and more.

She won RAW Artist of the Year in 2011, and performed at SXSW in 2019, Siren Nation Festival, and at the Crystal Ballroom for Folkfest. Strange & the Familiars released their first album, Pretty Deadly, in 2016. Soon after the first comic book collaboration between Stephanie Strange and artist Jay Reynolds was released, explicitly inviting listeners for the first time into the Strange World. Their second album, Strange World was released on Halloween 2021 and can be heard everywhere music is streamed.
