About

Stephanie Strange thought she was crazy when three black cats started talking to her. She’d had some moments in the past when she’d doubted her sanity, but when the Nightmares showed up in her dreams, that sealed the deal. Except it wasn’t just the cats, it was the tree outside her house, the spider spinning its web, and the crows that followed her to work. The Nightmares introduce her to the world of dreams and help her learn to walk further and further away from the waiting room, teaching her to protect herself from powerful fears that lurk in the shifting unconscious. She is charged by the Nightmares to share her cautionary observations with her fellow humans.

By the light of human dreams, the Nightmares lurk, helping to protect humans from their very own fears, coalesced into powerful demons and terrors, sabotaging their human hosts.

When The Preacher disappears, Ned and Nina are forced to confide in their newest confidante, Strange. A dark shadow is fast-approaching the world, consuming the potential of humankind, and even the Nightmares seem to fall powerless to it.

When Strange and the Nightmares are overtaken, and consequences bring reality to a screeching halt in the waking world, Strange questions her role in this looming battle. She begins to meet other women with powers beyond reason, and together they must figure out why they’ve been given these extraordinary gifts, and more importantly, how they will use them to help humans facing a point of no return, where their fears look to consume them.
